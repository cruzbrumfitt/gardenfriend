Privacy Policy

App: Garden Friend | Effective date: February 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Garden Friend handles information when you use the app.

1. Data Stored on Device

  • Garden tasks, reminders, and completion history.
  • Garden memories (photos, captions, notes) you add in the app.
  • App preferences and premium status cache.

This data is primarily stored locally on your device.

2. Data Sent to Third Parties When You Use Features

  • Plant identification: selected image data is sent to the configured identification service through the appâ€™s proxy endpoint.
  • Weather: city/location query data is sent to weather providers to return forecast information.
  • Purchases: subscription and purchase status is processed through Apple and RevenueCat services.

Third-party services may process data under their own terms and privacy policies.

3. Purchases and Billing

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple through your App Store account. We do not directly process your payment card information.

4. Notifications and Permissions

  • Local notifications are used for garden reminders if you grant notification permission.
  • Camera and photo library access are requested only when you choose to capture or select photos.

5. Data Retention and Control

  • You can clear local app data from in-app settings.
  • You can remove the app to delete app data stored on device, subject to platform behavior and backups.

6. Children

Garden Friend is a general-audience app (including users aged 9+). The app does not include account registration, and we do not knowingly collect personal data directly from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data through app features, contact us and we will review and address the request in accordance with applicable law.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions are effective when posted.

8. Contact
