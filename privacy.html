Privacy Policy

App: Garden Friend | Effective date: February 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Garden Friend handles information when you use the app.

1. Who We Are

Garden Friend is provided by Sidequestr.

Contact: sidequestr@proton.me

2. Quick Summary

  • Garden Friend primarily stores your data on your device.
  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use third-party ads or cross-app tracking.
  • Data is sent to third parties only when you use specific features (plant identification, weather, and purchases).

3. Information Stored on Your Device

  • Garden tasks, reminders, recurrence settings, and completion history.
  • Garden memories you create (photos, captions, notes, dates).
  • App preferences and state (including premium status cache and review prompt state).

This data remains on your device unless you delete it in app settings, uninstall the app, or your device/backups retain copies under platform behavior.

4. Information Sent to Third Parties When You Use Features

  • Plant Identification: When you choose a photo for identification, the image and selected plant-part parameter are sent to our proxy endpoint, which forwards the request to the configured identification provider (currently PlantNet).
  • Weather: When weather is shown, the app sends the selected city and its coordinates to the weather provider (currently Open-Meteo) to retrieve forecast data. The app does not request iOS location permission and does not collect precise GPS location from your device.
  • Purchases and Subscriptions: Payments are processed by Apple through your App Store account. Subscription and entitlement status are processed through RevenueCat and Apple to validate access to premium features. We do not directly receive or process your payment card details.
  • External Links: If you open support, legal, or subscription links, those websites process data under their own privacy policies.

5. How We Use Information

  • Provide core app features (tasks, reminders, memories, plant ID, weather).
  • Enable and restore premium access.
  • Operate app settings and user experience.
  • Respond to support requests you submit.

6. Notifications and Permissions

  • Notifications: Used for local garden reminders if you grant notification permission.
  • Camera: Requested only when you choose to take a photo.
  • Photo Library: Requested only when you choose to pick a photo.
  • Location: Not requested by this app.

You can change permissions at any time in your device settings.

7. Sharing, Sale, and Tracking

  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We do not use your data for third-party advertising.
  • We do not use cross-app tracking or request App Tracking Transparency permission.
  • We only share data with service providers as needed to deliver the features you use.

8. Data Retention and Your Control

  • You can clear local app data from in-app settings.
  • You can uninstall the app to remove app data from your device, subject to platform behavior and backups.
  • You can stop future data sharing for optional features by not using those features and/or revoking permissions.
  • For data handled by third-party providers, retention and deletion are governed by their own policies. Contact us and/or the provider if you need help with deletion requests.

9. Children

Garden Friend is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children through direct account registration in the app. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us so we can help address it.

10. International Processing

Third-party services used by the app may process data in countries other than your own. Processing is subject to those providers' terms and privacy practices.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions are effective when posted with a new effective date.

12. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to sidequestr@proton.me.
